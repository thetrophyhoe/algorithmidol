OFFICIAL RULES — ALGORITHM IDOL

“The Algorithm Saves You. The Humans Vote You Out.”
Presented by Glitched Records | Hosted by the Trophy Hoe

Season Overview

  • 10-week TikTok competition (Week 1 Purge + 8 competition weeks + live Finale).
  • 100% AI-generated music only, except for the one secret human contestant.
  • One secret human contestant (traditional DAW, zero AI) hidden among the 50 entrants.
  • All songs must be in English (80%+ lyrics).
  • Open to creators 18+. Identity verification required for prizes.

Week 1: The Great Purge (50 → 25)

  • Combined score elimination of Algorithm + Judges reduces the field from 50 to 25.
  • Artists are encouraged to leverage their network for engagement from day one.

Core Format (Weeks 2–10)

  1. Safety Round (Judges + Algorithm): Algorithm Score contributes 50% to the safety score. Top X are safe.
  2. Elimination Round (Pure Human Vote): Bottom group goes to public vote using #Save[name]week[number].

The Secret Human Twist — “Find the Glitch”

The human is treated exactly like every other contestant. No one knows who it is until the Finale. If the human makes the Top 3, the reveal happens live after the winner is announced.

Weekly Flow & Challenges

  • Tues-Wed: Submission window (24 hours).
  • Friday: Live judging session + safety reveal + NEXUS Glitch Power drop.
  • Weekend: Popular vote elimination opens Friday, closes Monday 12:01 AM PST.

*Challenges announced weekly on stream.

Prizes

  • Weekly: Spotlights, judge shoutouts, playlist adds.
  • Finale Winner: Cash + lifetime Suno membership + Glitched Records merch + full promo push.
